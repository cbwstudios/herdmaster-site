Billing questions.

No trial needed, the free plan is permanent. Track up to 10 animals with full breeding records, no time limit and no credit card, and upgrade when you outgrow it.

Subscriptions are managed through your Apple ID, cancel anytime from Settings on your iPhone. You keep access until the end of the current billing period.

Your data is never deleted. Drop to a lower tier and everything stays, you just can't add new records past that tier's limits until you upgrade. Export anytime, we don't lock in your data.

Purchases are processed by Apple, so refunds go through Apple's process at reportaproblem.apple.com.
