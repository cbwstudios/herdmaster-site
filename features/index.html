Features

From the herd list to Grand Champion legs.

Built for rabbit and cavy breeders. Open the app to live counts of your animals, open dams, active breedings, and recent litters, and every feature below hangs off that same workflow.

  • Herd. Every animal in one searchable, breed-validated list.
  • Pedigrees. Four generations, printable and shareable.
  • Breeding. Dam-first pairings with the full timeline built in.
  • Litters & kits. From the nest box to a permanent record.
  • Health. Recurring tasks, treatments, and weight history.
  • Shows. Every leg tracked toward Grand Champion.
  • Sales & payments. Customers, sale records, and payment requests by Venmo or PayPal, kept on the animal.
  • Imports. One-pass Evans XML and scan-and-confirm OCR.
  • Offline + iCloud. Works with no signal, syncs privately.

01 · Herd Management

Every animal, every breed,
one organized list.

Your whole herd lives in one searchable list, grouped into collapsible breed sections with live buck and doe counts, so a barn with three breeds reads as easily as a barn with one.

  • Complete records. Photos, statuses, barn names, genotype fields, and cull reasons on every animal.
  • Breed and variety validation. 53 rabbit and 13 cavy breeds built in, so every entry stays show-accurate.
  • Find anything fast. Search by name, ear number, or tattoo, with long-press quick actions on any row.

03 · Breeding

Plan every pairing,
organized by doe.

One buck breeds many does, so HerdMaster organizes breedings the way a rabbitry actually runs: by doe. Pair a sire and dam, and the whole timeline builds itself.

  • The full timeline. Palpation windows, nest box timing, and expected kindle dates, all from one breeding date.
  • Moon phase calendar. Breeders who plan by the moon get the phases built right into the picker.
  • Duplicate detection. Get flagged before a duplicate breeding slips into the records.
  • History that follows the doe. Past breedings and status updates stay right on her record.

04 · Litters & Kit Tracking

From the nest box to a permanent record.

Log the litter the day it arrives, then track every kit through weaning: counts, sexes, and outcomes stay current without a paper tally in the barn.

  • Full litter records. Born dates and kit counts, with alive, deceased, and culled tracked separately, including cull reasons.
  • Show-quality designations. Mark the keepers early and watch the promising ones develop.
  • Weaning summaries. A clean summary of the litter when it leaves the nest box.
  • Tattoo promotion. Promote a kit to a permanent herd record with its tattoo and ear number, history intact.

05 · Health Records

Set it once, never
miss a treatment.

Set a health task once and let it recur on its own. Custom frequencies, repeat-until scheduling, and calendar recurrence keep the whole barn on protocol without a wall calendar.

  • Custom frequencies. Daily, weekly, or whatever schedule your protocol calls for.
  • Repeat-until scheduling. Treatments end when they should, not when you remember.
  • Health tests on record. Log tests alongside treatments, so the full history lives with the animal.
  • Weight tracking. Quick weight entry with the full history charted per animal.

Count every leg
toward Grand Champion.

  • Judge names, optional. Note the judge when it's worth remembering.

08 · Imports & Migration

Your whole herd, moved
in one pass.

A full Evans XML import brings animals, winnings, and litters across in a single pass, and a paper pedigree is one photo away with scan-and-confirm OCR.

  • Evans XML, the lead path. The entire export comes across: animals, winnings, and litters, one pass.
  • OCR pedigree scan. Photo, PDF, or scan; HerdMaster reads it in and you review and confirm every field.
  • Every other road in. Evans PDF, Everbreed Share Link, Everbreed Litters CSV, XPEDX and HTM fallbacks, and manual entry.
  • Safe by design. Autofill suggestions, import summaries, duplicate handling, and rollback if an import fails.

09 · Offline & iCloud Sync

Offline-first, synced through
your own iCloud.

HerdMaster works fully offline, so you can add litters, log weights, and update records in the barn or at a show with no signal. Back online, everything syncs privately through your own iCloud, with no third-party server holding your records.
