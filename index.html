Your rabbits. Your barn. In your pocket.

HerdMaster was built by a 45-year breeder for the real rhythm of a rabbitry: out at the hutches, at the nest box, at the show table. It works the way you do, in your hand and fully offline, so recordkeeping happens where the animals are, not back at the computer.

The rabbits.

Every doe, buck, and kit, with photos, pedigrees, varieties, and show wins.

The barn.

Log breedings, nest-box dates, palpation windows, and kindle dates right where you stand.

In your hand.

However your records live today, there's a fast path in.

Evans XML import

Animals, winnings, and litters all come across in one pass. Most tools only import the subject rabbit and leave you to type in the ancestors. HerdMaster brings the full pedigree.

OCR pedigree scan

Snap or upload a paper pedigree as a photo, PDF, or scan, and HerdMaster reads it in for you to review and confirm.

Evans PDF Everbreed Share Link Everbreed Litters CSV Evans XPEDX / HTM Manual entry
Every import checks for duplicates before saving, and you can roll back a failed import without touching your existing records.

Your herd data is yours.

Your records live in your own private iCloud, not on our servers, and they're yours to export any time.

Offline is the default.

Every feature works with no signal at all. Out in the barn, in a metal shed, or anywhere off the grid, the app keeps working exactly the same. Sync happens later, on its own, when you're back online.

Saved to your iCloud, not a server.

Your records sync through Apple's CloudKit on your own personal iCloud, not to any HerdMaster server.

No lock-in, ever.

Your data is never trapped in the app. Export it and take it to another system any time you like.
