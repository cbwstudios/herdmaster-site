All your animals. In your hand. Even offline.

HERDMASTER RABBIT & CAVY was created by Joe Kim, an ARBA Convention Best in Show Winner and a life member of the American Rabbit Breeders Association. It was created to be an easy-to-use, powerful solution for the serious rabbit and cavy breeder.

All your rabbits

Every buck, doe, boar, and sow, with photos, pedigrees, breeding records, and show wins.

In your barn

Log breedings, nest-box dates, palpation dates, and kindle dates in the barn, even offline.

In your hand

Works fully offline. The data is yours and lives on your iCloud, not someone else's servers.

From the nest box to the show table, HERDMASTER RABBIT & CAVY keeps you covered.

Everything in one app

Built for serious rabbit and cavy breeders.

Herd, pedigrees, breeding, litters, health, shows, and sales stay connected in one app, instead of scattered across notebooks, spreadsheets, and a drawer full of paper pedigrees.

Herd on Record

Every buck, doe, boar, and sow in one searchable list, grouped by breed.

  • Collapsible breed sections with live buck and doe counts
  • 53 rabbit and 13 cavy breeds, with variety validation built in
  • Search by name, ear number, or tattoo

Pedigrees and Imports

Move your whole herd across, then view and share clean four-generation pedigrees.

  • Evans XML brings animals, winnings, and litters in a single pass
  • Scan a paper pedigree and confirm every field, no guesswork
  • Four-generation trees you can print or share as a PDF

Breeding and Litters

Pair a sire and dam and the whole timeline builds itself, right through to the kits.

  • Bred, palpate, nest, and due dates from one breeding date
  • Breedings organized by doe, since one buck covers many
  • Track kits as alive, deceased, or keeper, then promote on tattoo

Health and Weights

Schedule a health task once and let it recur, with weights charted per animal.

  • Vet check, health check, or treatment on any animal's record
  • Custom frequencies and repeat-until scheduling
  • Weight entry with the full history per animal

Shows and Grand Champion

Log a win at the show table and it lands where it counts, legs adding up toward the title.

  • Grand Champion leg tracking, so you see exactly where each animal stands
  • Multiple awards per show entry, with the primary win flagged
  • Judge and class recorded on every result

Sales and Payments

Keep customers, sales, and payment requests next to the records they belong to.

  • Customers, sale records, and a buyer waitlist in one place
  • Recurring orders for the buyers who come back
  • Request payment by Venmo or PayPal and record the status

Offline is the default.

Every feature works with no signal at all. Out in the barn, in a metal shed, or anywhere off the grid, the app keeps working exactly the same. Sync happens later, on its own, when you're back online.

Saved to your iCloud, not a server.

Your records sync through Apple's CloudKit on your own personal iCloud, not to any HerdMaster server.

No lock-in, ever.

Your data is never trapped in the app. Export it and take it to another system any time you like.
